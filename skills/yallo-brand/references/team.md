## Email Signature Structure

```
[2px gold gradient bar top]
[photo 62×62px, gold border 1.5px, border-radius 4px] | [gold vertical line] | [name/title/company/contacts]
```

### Layout rules
- Photo cell: 72px wide, padding-right 14px
- Vertical gold line: 1px, gradient fade, min-height 74px
- Info cell: vertical-align top
- Name: 15px, weight 700, #111, letter-spacing -.3px
- Title: 11px, #777777
- Company: 11px, weight 700, #D4A843, letter-spacing .05em, margin-bottom 8px
- Separator: 1px #e8e8e8, margin-bottom 8px
- Contact lines: 11px, #555555, line-height 1.8
- Labels (m/e/w): #c0c0c0, 10px, min-width 14px
- Links: #D4A843, no underline
- Credentials: 10px, #aaaaaa, italic

### Contact line format (one line)
`yallo.co · linkedin.com/in/[handle] · [Location]`

### Raphy special rule
Specialisation line goes at BOTTOM — after all contact details.
Style: `font-size:10px; color:#D4A843; font-style:italic; border-top:1px solid #f0f0f0; padding-top:6px; margin-top:7px`

### Initials fallback box (when no photo)
`width:62px; height:62px; border-radius:4px; border:1.5px solid #D4A843; background:#f2f2f0; font-size:20px; font-weight:700; color:#D4A843; font-family:Arial`
