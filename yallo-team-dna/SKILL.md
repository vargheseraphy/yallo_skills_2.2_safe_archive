## SECTION 1 — YALLO AT A GLANCE

YALLO Group is a specialist technology strategy and talent consulting firm
that bridges the gap between tech strategy and execution for enterprise IT
leaders globally.

**Three Service Pillars**
1. TS/EA as a Service — Tech Strategy and Enterprise Architecture consulting
2. Talent in a Box — Specialist tech talent delivery (contract and permanent)
3. Managed IT CoE — Application support and managed services

**Who YALLO Serves**
CIOs, CTOs, IT Directors, VP Engineering, HR Leaders (talent mandates),
Finance Leaders (cost and ROI conversations).

**Industries**
Retail and Consumer Goods, BFSI, Healthcare and Life Sciences, Public Sector,
Manufacturing and Logistics, Telco and Media.

**Primary Geographies**
UAE/GCC (primary growth market), UK (origin market), India (talent delivery base).

**Core Differentiator**
Unlike pure IT consulting firms (strategy only) or pure recruitment firms
(bodies only) — YALLO integrates both. Strategy and talent unified.

### saasinator — AI Systems Integrator (SI for AI)

**Brand status:** Independent brand. YALLO Group company. Domain: saasinator.ai
**Brand orchestrator / web owner:** Raphy Varghese
**Category:** Services-led AI systems integrator — not a SaaS product, not a platform.
**Position:** "We rebuild, build, and replace the enterprise software you rent —
as Claude-native systems you own. Cheaper. Better. Faster."

**Three engagement types:**
- **IGNITE** — Build net-new AI-native capability
- **REFORGE** — Rebuild existing legacy systems (.NET, Java, Oracle APEX) AI-native
- **LIBERATE** — Replace enterprise SaaS contracts (SAP, Oracle, Salesforce, Workday,
  Blue Yonder) with owned Claude-native equivalents

**Methodology:** SAIF (Saasinator AI Factory). Three stages: Brief → Build → Deploy.
Glass-walled — every step visible, logged, auditable. Tests first, then code, then evals.
Client leaves with the system, the source, and the runbook.
SAIF methodology detail stays **internal** — Brief → Build → Deploy is what goes external.

**Tech stack:** Anthropic Claude (no wrappers), Nuxt 3, Tailwind CSS, Vercel,
Plus Jakarta Sans, DM Mono. CRM: HubSpot + Calendly. Outreach: Apollo.
Analytics: GA4 + GTM. Email: Acumbamail. Design: Figma + Canva. PM: Notion.

**Who buys it:** CIO, CFO, CTO at $1B+ revenue enterprises in UAE and KSA.
Priority sectors: Financial Services, Retail, Oil & Gas, Government, Manufacturing.

**Proof points:**
- YALLO Hub (REFORGE) — rebuilt YALLO's own ops Claude-native; 35 screens,
  replaces HubSpot + Apollo + Vincere
- MENA retail group (LIBERATE) — 70+ brands, core platform replaced in 30 days,
  $0 licence post-delivery

**Brand rules (non-negotiable):**
- Always written **saasinator** — lowercase, never "Saasinator"
- No pricing anywhere on site or in content
- No unverified statistics
